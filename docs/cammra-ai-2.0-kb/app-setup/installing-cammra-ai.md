# Installing CAMMRA AI

To access and manage applications on the camera's webpage, follow these steps:

1. Open the Camera's Webpage.
2. Go to **Apps** menu.
3. In the Apps menu click **+Add app**
4. Select the CAMMRA AI Application installation file on your PC.
5. Once CAMMRA AI is installed, please activate the license:
   - Click the **three dots** menu and choose **"Activate license automatically"**.
   - Enter your license code and click **"Activate"**
6. Click on the **Open** or **Launch** button (the specific label may vary depending on the camera's interface).

By following these steps, you can easily access the camera's webpage, navigate to the application settings, locate the CAMMRA AI application, and open it for further configuration or use.
